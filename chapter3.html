Chapter 3: The Republic of Fish Consumers (RFC) is established

This went on for some time, but eventually the penguins were starting to seriously suffer.

While the cats could feast as much as they pleased, the penguins hardly had enough food to go around.

One day it reached a boiling point when a chick succumbed from malnutrition. A penguin who would

become known as Comrade Flippers, decided that enough was enough. So he began to to organize with

the other penguins, pointing out how they have strength in numbers. They developed a strategy in

which they would slide around the base of wherever the cats would be posted, thus forcing the felines

to meet the penguins' demands. After some redistribution the cats and penguins lived in harmony

working to improve society for all species
